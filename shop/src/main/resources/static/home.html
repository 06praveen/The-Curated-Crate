Our Mission

Craft is not a job.
It's an identity.

Across India's villages, 7 million artisans practice skills that took generations to perfect. Yet most earn less than ₹300 a day — while their work sells for 10x that in urban stores.

The Curated Crate was built to close that gap. We work directly with maker communities, pay transparent fair wages, and tell the full story behind every product.
